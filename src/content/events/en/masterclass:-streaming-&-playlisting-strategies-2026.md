How do you break through the noise on platforms like Spotify, Apple Music, and TikTok in 2026? This half-day masterclass provides the tools to build a sustainable streaming strategy without losing your artistic integrity.

> We will explore how algorithms have evolved and the best ways to collaborate with curators and editors.

## What’s included?

* Deep dive into the 2026 algorithm updates
* Pitching strategies: How to grab the editors' attention
* Data analysis: Identifying your core audience and their location
* Workshop: Creating a release plan for your next single
* Networking and knowledge sharing
